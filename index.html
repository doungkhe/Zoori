Meet the Stars of the Wild

Get ready to meet some of the coolest animals on the planet! Dive in and discover what makes each of these creatures special.

Lion

Lions are unique among cats because they are the only ones to live in social groups called prides.

Monkey

Monkeys are fascinating creatures known for mutual grooming, which strengthens their social bonds and shows affection.

Hippopotamus

Hippos, also called river horses, are large, semi-aquatic African mammals known for swimming and their love of water.
